## Notable Mechanics

**Multi-hit spells** (Flame Burst, Chain Lightning, Whirlpool, Storm Call, etc.): each individual hit triggers type effectiveness and Root detonation separately.

**Zero-cost spells** (Kindle, Mana Spring, Overcharge, Chill, Tremor, Seed Shot, Mana Petal, Purify): free to cast, but most have trade-offs (discard, Fade, minor values).

**Fade cards**: one-use only, removed from deck on play. Starter Fade cards (Focus, Guard, Amplify, Mana Petal) naturally thin from deck as used. Non-starter Fade spells are clearly marked.

**Strength scaling**: Dark Pact *(S)* (Shadow) and Magma Form, Earthen Skin, Radiance all grant permanent Strength — compounding on every subsequent attack.
