Status effects, mechanics, and how they interact

Type-Specific Status Effects

🔥
Char Fire
Char stacks accumulate on the enemy. At the end of each of its turns, it takes damage equal to its current Char stacks. Then remove 1 Char stack.
End-of-turn damage Decays −1/turn Stacks = damage dealt
❄️
Freeze Ice
Freeze stacks accumulate on the enemy. At 3 stacks, it becomes Frozen — it skips its next action and all Freeze stacks are removed. Freeze stacks never decay on their own.
Skips action at 3 stacks Never decays All stacks consumed on trigger
⚡
Shock Lightning
Shock stacks accumulate on the enemy. While it has any Shock stacks, Lightning spells deal ×1.25 bonus damage per stack (exponential). Remove 1 Shock stack at the end of each of its turns.
Lightning ×1.25 per stack Decays −1/turn Stacks = multiplier & duration
🌊
Drown Water
Drown stacks accumulate on the enemy. At the start of each of its turns, it takes damage equal to its current Drown stacks. Drown does not decay on its own.
Start-of-turn damage Never decays Stacks = damage dealt
💫
Daze Rock
Daze stacks accumulate on the enemy. While it has any Daze stacks, it has a 50% chance to repeat its previous action instead of its next one. Remove 1 Daze stack at the end of each of its turns.
50% action repeat Decays −1/turn Stacks = duration
👁
Blind Light
Blind stacks accumulate on the enemy. While it has any Blind stacks, each of its attacks has a 50% chance to miss entirely. Remove 1 Blind stack at the end of each of its turns.
50% miss chance Decays −1/turn Stacks = duration
🌿
Root Grass
Root stacks accumulate on the enemy. When it takes damage, all Root stacks are consumed and it takes damage equal to twice the removed stacks.
Detonates on damage 2× stacks as burst All stacks consumed on trigger
🩸
Lifesteal Shadow
Lifesteal stacks accumulate on the enemy. At the end of its next turn, all stacks trigger at once — it loses that many HP and you heal for the same amount. All stacks are then removed.
Damage + heal Fires end of enemy turn All stacks consumed

Shared Status Effects

💔
Weak Shared
Weak stacks accumulate on the target. While it has any Weak stacks, its outgoing damage is reduced by 25%. Remove 1 Weak stack at the end of each of its turns.
−25% damage dealt Decays −1/turn Stacks = duration
💪
Strength Shared
Strength stacks accumulate on the holder. While it has any Strength stacks, its damage and block are each increased by an amount equal to its current Strength stacks. Remove 1 Strength stack at the end of each of its turns.
+N damage & block Decays −1/turn Affects holder
