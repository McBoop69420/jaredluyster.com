Keyword Reference

Status effects, mechanics, and how they interact

Type-Specific Status Effects

🔥
Char Fire
At the start of each turn, the target takes damage equal to its current Char stacks. Then the stack count decreases by 1.
Damage over time Decays −1/turn Stacks = damage dealt
❄️
Freeze Ice
Freeze stacks accumulate on the enemy. When they reach 3 stacks, the enemy is Frozen — their action is skipped entirely and all Freeze stacks are consumed. Below 3 stacks, Freeze decays by 1 each turn. Example: 2 stacks today → decays to 1. Apply 1 more → hits 3 → Frozen.
Skips action at 3 stacks Decays −1 below threshold All stacks consumed on trigger
⚡
Shock Lightning
While the target has Shock stacks, Lightning spells deal ×1.25 per stack bonus damage (exponential). Stacks decay by 1 each turn. Example: Shock 1 → ×1.25. Shock 2 → ×1.56. Shock 3 → ×1.95.
Lightning ×1.25 per stack Decays −1/turn Stacks = multiplier & duration
🌊
Drown Water
At the start of each turn, the target takes damage equal to its current Drown stacks. Then the stack count decreases by 1.
Damage over time Decays −1/turn Stacks = damage dealt
💫
Daze Rock
The target's attacks deal flat damage equal to current Daze stacks less than normal. As stacks decay each turn, the reduction shrinks alongside them. Example: Daze 3 → enemy hits for −3 this turn, −2 next, −1 after.
Flat dmg reduction Decays −1/turn Stacks = reduction & duration
👁
Blind Light
While the target has any Blind stacks, each of their attacks has a 50% chance to deal 0 damage (miss entirely). Stack count decays by 1 each turn.
50% miss chance Decays −1/turn Stacks = duration
🌿
Root Grass
At the start of each turn, the target takes damage equal to its current Root stacks. Then the stack count decreases by 1.
Damage over time Decays −1/turn Stacks = damage dealt
🩸
Lifesteal Shadow
Applied to the enemy as a status. At the end of the enemy's next turn, all stacks are consumed: the enemy loses that many HP and the player heals for the same amount. Stacks do not decay each turn — they all fire at once, then disappear.
Damage + heal Fires end of enemy turn All stacks consumed

Shared Status Effects

💔
Weak Shared
The target's outgoing damage is reduced by 25% (multiplicative). Stacks represent duration — each turn the stack count decreases by 1.
−25% damage dealt Decays −1/turn Stacks = duration
💪
Strength Shared
The holder's damage and block are each increased by a flat amount equal to the stack count. Stacks represent duration — decreases by 1 each turn.
+N damage & block Decays −1/turn Affects holder
